About Me

My name is Stella Kaval and I'm a 2nd year Computer Science major at UC Berkeley. This summer, I will be joining Oracle in Redwood City, CA as a Software Engineering Intern in Oracle Cloud Platforms!

One of the things I'm most passionate about is teaching and sharing my knowledge with others. I've had the chance to do this as a programming instructor and a math tutor, and I've really enjoyed being able to help students understand complex concepts and see their progress. I've also had the opportunity to take on a leadership role as the Lead Director of CS Kickstart, where I organized and executed an annual introductory CS program for 100+ female-identifying students at UC Berkeley.

I've also worked on a number of personal projects, which have helped me continue learning and improving my skills. I'm always looking for new opportunities to learn and grow, both personally and professionally. I'm passionate about using my skills and knowledge to make a positive impact in the world and I'm excited to see what the future holds!
